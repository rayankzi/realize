# UCI Summer Research Fellowship: Computing & AI for All Undergrad REU

**Topic/Category:** Education / Research Opportunities
**Primary Goal of the Post:** To inform undergraduate students about a paid summer research fellowship at UC Irvine focused on developing AI curriculum for middle school students.

---

## Executive Summary

This post promotes the UCI Computing & AI for All REU (Research Experience for Undergraduates) program for summer 2026. The opportunity offers a $10,000 NSF-funded stipend for undergraduates to work full-time at UC Irvine developing curricular materials that integrate AI into middle school science and computer science instruction. The application deadline is April 3, 2026.

## Key Insights & Main Ideas

- **Paid Research Opportunity:** Fellows receive a $10,000 stipend funded by the NSF Research Experience for Undergraduates Program.
- **Focus Area:** Developing curricular materials for integrating AI into science and computer science instruction for middle school students.
- **Duration & Location:** Full-time, on-campus work at UC Irvine for 10 of 12 weeks between June 8 and August 28, 2026 (schedule is negotiable).
- **Eligibility Restriction:** Applicants must be U.S. citizens, U.S. nationals, or permanent residents due to NSF requirements.
- **Application Deadline:** April 3, 2026.
