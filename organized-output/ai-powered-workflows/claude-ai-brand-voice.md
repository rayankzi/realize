# Stop Using Claude AI Like ChatGPT: How to Program It for Your Brand Voice

**Topic/Category:** AI / Content Creation / Brand Strategy
**Primary Goal of the Post:** Teach content creators and entrepreneurs how to properly set up Claude AI with their brand DNA so it produces personalized, on-brand content instead of generic AI-sounding output.

---

## Executive Summary

This carousel argues that most people underutilize Claude AI by treating it identically to ChatGPT — typing vague, context-free prompts and getting generic results. The key shift is to "program" Claude with your brand guidelines, tone, transformation story, and examples of past high-performing content before asking it to create anything. When properly configured, Claude becomes a second brain that executes your strategy while maintaining your unique voice, rather than a generic writing tool that sounds like everyone else.

## Key Insights & Main Ideas

- **Claude is not ChatGPT — it requires different setup.** Most users apply the same generic prompting habits across all AI tools, which leads to mediocre, robotic output regardless of the platform.
- **Brand DNA must come before content requests.** Before asking Claude to write anything, you need to feed it your brand guidelines, tone of voice, examples of successful past posts, and your transformation story.
- **Use proven frameworks, not blank-slate prompts.** Instead of asking Claude to create from scratch, upload posts that have worked, have it extract the framework, and then request variations targeting different pain points.
- **AI doesn't replace expertise — it executes strategy.** The human provides the personality, proof points, and strategic direction; Claude handles the structural heavy lifting.
- **Proper programming eliminates the "AI sound."** When Claude is correctly set up with your voice and frameworks, the output sounds like you, not like a machine.
