# 5 Claude Workflows That Save 4 Hours a Day

**Topic/Category:** AI / Business Automation
**Primary Goal of the Post:** Showcase five specific Claude-powered workflows that automate repetitive business tasks — from learning new skills to content planning — positioning Claude as a comprehensive business co-worker, not just a chatbot.

---

## Executive Summary

Creator Adriana Bubori demonstrates how she uses "Claude Cowork" — a suite of Claude-powered automated workflows — to save four hours daily in her business. The carousel walks through five distinct workflows: a Knowledge Analyzer for learning from YouTube videos, a News Radar for automated weekly industry briefings, a Content Planner that generates two weeks of ideas and populates a Notion calendar, a Project Manager that reviews Notion projects and identifies bottlenecks before team meetings, and a Brief to Script Writer that turns content briefs into full scripts. Each workflow is shown with real screenshots of the tools in action, emphasizing full automation and Notion integration.

## Key Insights & Main Ideas

- **Claude is a business co-worker, not a chatbot:** The post reframes Claude from a simple Q&A tool into a multi-workflow automation engine that handles research, planning, reporting, and content creation.
- **Automation + Notion integration is the backbone:** Nearly every workflow feeds into or pulls from Notion — calendars, project boards, and databases — making it a centralized hub.
- **Scheduled/recurring tasks are key:** Workflows like News Radar run on a weekly schedule (every Monday at 8:05 AM), fully automated without manual triggering.
- **Content creation at scale:** Two weeks of content ideas can be generated, categorized by content pillars and funnel stages, and placed directly into a filming calendar.
- **Learning acceleration:** Claude can break down any YouTube video into step-by-step lessons tailored to the user's industry, making skill acquisition faster and more relevant.
