## Executive Summary

This carousel post presents a practical toolkit of 7 Claude AI prompts designed to transform every stage of the job search process — from resume writing to networking. The core thesis is that modern job hunting isn't about volume (applying to 100 jobs) but about precision (applying strategically to 10). Each prompt is crafted to make the user adopt the mindset of a recruiter, ensuring their materials are results-driven, recruiter-optimized, and compelling. The caption reinforces this with a real-world anecdote of someone who landed multiple interviews in a single week by switching from generic applications to AI-tailored outreach.

## Key Insights & Main Ideas

- **Quality over quantity:** The post advocates applying to fewer jobs with highly tailored materials rather than mass-applying with a generic resume.
- **Recruiter-perspective prompting:** Every prompt instructs Claude to "act as a recruiter" or "act as a hiring manager," forcing output that aligns with what decision-makers actually look for.
- **Full-funnel coverage:** The 7 prompts cover the entire job search pipeline — resume, LinkedIn, interview prep, job search planning, cold DMs, follow-ups, and networking.
- **Measurable impact focus:** Prompts emphasize quantified achievements, business value, and results-driven language over generic responsibilities.
- **Low-friction outreach:** Cold message and networking prompts are designed to be concise (under 80-100 words) with clear, low-pressure calls to action.
