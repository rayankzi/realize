# How to Build a Self-Posting Content Repurposing System in One Afternoon

**Topic/Category:** Content Automation / Social Media Strategy
**Primary Goal of the Post:** Teach creators how to set up an automated content repurposing system using Make.com, Airtable/Notion, and ChatGPT — matched to their personal content creation style.

---

## Executive Summary

This carousel walks creators through the process of building a "set-it-and-forget-it" content repurposing system. The core idea is that prolific creators aren't manually posting everywhere — they have a system. The post helps you identify your natural content creation style (writer, speaker, fast creator, or system builder), match it to a repurposing "engine," and then wire up Make.com + Airtable to automate the distribution of content across platforms. The promise: one idea becomes 3-8 content assets with minimal ongoing effort.

## Key Insights & Main Ideas

- **Prolific creators use systems, not effort:** The reason some people seem to post everywhere constantly is that they've automated the repurposing pipeline, not because they're manually reposting.
- **Match your engine to your style:** There are four repurposing engines (Newsletter, Transcript, Momentum, Leverage), each suited to a different type of creator. Choosing the right one reduces friction.
- **Start simple — repurpose old posts first:** The easiest entry point is building an automation that takes an existing post from your social media planner and schedules it on another platform.
- **1 idea = 3-8 assets:** Depending on the engine, a single piece of content can be broken into 3-8 platform-specific assets automatically.
- **Tools needed are accessible:** Make.com (automation), Airtable or Notion (content storage/hub), and ChatGPT (content transformation) form the core stack.
