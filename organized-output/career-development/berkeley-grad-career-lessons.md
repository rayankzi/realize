## Core Insights & Takeaways

- **Networking Follow-Up Hack:** Ask a contact for their favorite book or podcast, consume it, then reference it in your next conversation — an organic, low-effort way to stay top of mind.
- **GPA Is Overrated (Usually):** Unless you're heading to grad school or fields like investment banking, real-world experience outweighs GPA every time.
- **Don't Drop Out for a Startup:** Especially as a woman, a college degree serves as an equalizer — the startup can wait.
- **AI-Proof Your Career:** Build a niche at the intersection of multiple fields. Being "multi-passionate" creates a skillset that's hard for AI to replicate.
- **Visibility = Opportunity:** Personal branding is the easiest lever for generating career opportunities.
- **Your Environment Determines Your Success:** Surround yourself with ambitious, goal-oriented people.
- **Network Laterally, Not Just Upward:** People one or two years ahead of you are more plugged into current industry realities than C-suite executives decades removed from your stage.
- **Study Smarter, Not Fancier:** Ditch elaborate study methods — active recall through flashcards or practice problems is all you need.
