# The 90-Day Zero-to-Job Roadmap for Data Analysts in 2026

**Topic/Category:** Data Analytics / Career
**Primary Goal of the Post:** A comprehensive, week-by-week action plan for someone with zero work experience to land their first data analyst job (targeting $55-80K) within 90 days by mastering SQL, Excel, Power BI, and Python, building a portfolio, and executing a disciplined job search.

---

## Executive Summary

This carousel lays out an extremely detailed 90-day blueprint for breaking into data analytics with no prior experience. The strategy is built on three pillars: skills mastery and portfolio building (Month 1), applications and networking (Month 2), and interview domination (Month 3). The post emphasizes execution over perfection — committing just 2 hours per day across a structured learning order (SQL first, then Excel, Power BI, and Python), building 3 live portfolio projects using free Kaggle datasets, and aggressively applying (50 apps/week) while networking on LinkedIn. It includes a copy-paste resume template, a LinkedIn outreach DM script, behavioral STAR story frameworks, and a job application tracking spreadsheet format.

## Key Insights & Main Ideas

- **SQL is the #1 priority:** 80% of interviews test SQL first. Learn it in weeks 1-2 before anything else.
- **Only 2 hours/day required:** The entire plan runs on a daily 2-hour commitment (270 total hours over 90 days).
- **3 live portfolio projects beat 1 perfect one:** Winners have "3 live projects + links everywhere" vs. wannabes who obsess over a single project.
- **Volume over perfection in applications:** Target 50 apps/week with a "good resume + 500 apps" mindset rather than spending 2 months perfecting a resume.
- **Python is a salary booster (+20-30%)** but not the first thing to learn — it comes in Phase 4 after SQL, Excel, and Power BI.
- **Networking is a numbers game:** Send 10 LinkedIn DMs/day to get 2 coffee chats/week and 1 referral.
- **Expected outcome:** 90 days x 2 hrs/day = SQL fluency + 3 portfolio projects + 500 applications = 50 interviews → 5 offers → $70K first job.
