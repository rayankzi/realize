# How to Stay Ahead of 99% of Engineers: Habits from a Microsoft SDE

**Topic/Category:** Tech / Career Development
**Primary Goal of the Post:** A Microsoft Software Development Engineer shares the key habits and strategies that help him stay ahead of the vast majority of engineers in the industry.

---

## Executive Summary

This carousel from a Microsoft SDE outlines four core habits that he credits for staying ahead of 99% of engineers: leveraging AI tools for productivity, continuously practicing DSA and system design, maintaining an updated LinkedIn presence, and cultivating a confident communication style. The post blends practical technical advice with personal branding and mindset tips, targeting early-to-mid career engineers looking to differentiate themselves.

## Key Insights & Main Ideas

- **Insight 1:** AI tools are a massive, underutilized advantage — most engineers are unaware of how many AI tools exist that can streamline their workflow across any field.
- **Insight 2:** Data Structures & Algorithms (DSA) and System Design remain the most important technical foundations, even for working engineers. Consistent practice (not just during interview prep) is key.
- **Insight 3:** Keeping your LinkedIn profile updated with achievements, experience, and featured posts (especially coding profiles) helps your profile stand out to recruiters and peers.
- **Insight 4:** Confidence and communication — speaking with authority — is a differentiator that compounds with technical achievement.
