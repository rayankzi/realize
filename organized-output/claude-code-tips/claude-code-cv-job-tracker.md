# Upgrade Your CV and Track Job Applications with Claude Code

**Topic/Category:** Career Development / AI-Powered Job Search
**Primary Goal of the Reel:** Teach viewers how to use Claude Code to optimize their CV for industry relevance and set up an automated job application tracker.

---

## Executive Summary

This reel walks through a practical workflow for using Claude Code as a job search assistant. The creator demonstrates a two-part system: first, using Claude to audit and upgrade your CV so it speaks directly to current industry problems, and second, setting up a lightweight job application tracker that logs contacts, follow-ups, and application statuses — all managed through conversational prompts to Claude.

## Core Insights & Takeaways

- **Insight 1:** The key to getting your CV noticed is aligning it with current industry problems — not just listing past experience. Claude can generate a report on the problems your target industry faces at your level, then help you retrofit your CV to mirror that language.
- **Insight 2:** Use voice notes to describe your past experience naturally. Claude can synthesize spoken descriptions and update the language in your CV roles to match industry relevance — while keeping it human-sounding and free of AI slop.
- **Insight 3:** After updating your CV, have Claude reconcile it against the job market report to ensure your language directly speaks to the identified problems.
- **Insight 4:** In about two minutes, you can set up a job application tracker in Claude that logs jobs applied for, contacts spoken to, follow-up reminders, and action items — all updated conversationally.

## Narrative & Step-by-Step Breakdown

1. **Generate an Industry Report:** Give Claude your CV, target industry, and desired position level. Ask it to compile a report on the current problems the industry is facing.
2. **Retrofit Your CV:** Use the report to update each relevant past position so it demonstrates you've solved (or have tangible experience with) those problems. Send voice notes describing your experience so Claude can synthesize natural language.
3. **Rewrite in Your Own Voice:** Review Claude's output and rewrite it in your own language to avoid AI-sounding copy. The goal is relevance + authenticity.
4. **Reconcile CV Against Report:** Have Claude cross-check the updated CV against the industry report to confirm alignment.
5. **Build a Job Tracker:** Ask Claude to create a tracker for jobs applied, people contacted, and follow-up status. Update it with each new application — provide links, contacts, and status.
6. **Use Follow-Up Prompts:** At any time, ask Claude "Do I need to follow up with anyone?" to get a list of pending actions.
