## Executive Summary

This carousel is a comprehensive content idea vault for Instagram creators, offering 200 reel prompts organized into three strategic categories: Beginners & How-To, Storytelling & Mindset, and Tips, Tools & Hacks. Each prompt is a fill-in-the-blank template that creators can personalize to their niche, making it an endlessly reusable resource. ## Notable Visual Context

The entire carousel uses a handwritten font on plain white/off-white paper backgrounds, giving it a personal, notebook-style aesthetic. This is a deliberate design choice — it feels approachable and authentic rather than overly designed, which aligns with the "show your human side" messaging. The simplicity also makes the text the star, ensuring the prompts are easy to read and screenshot/save. The cover and CTA slides use larger handwriting for emphasis, while the list slides use smaller, denser text to pack maximum value per slide.
