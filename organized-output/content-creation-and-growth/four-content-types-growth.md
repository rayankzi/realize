# 4 Types of Content You Need to Grow on Social Media

**Topic/Category:** Content Strategy / Social Media Marketing
**Primary Goal of the Post:** Teach creators and business owners the four essential content pillars — Growth, Connection, Authority, and Conversion — and how to use each strategically to attract followers, build trust, and drive sales.

---

## Executive Summary

This carousel breaks down a complete social media content strategy into four distinct content types, each serving a specific purpose in the audience journey. The creator explains that most people fail because they only post one type of content. By intentionally mixing Growth, Connection, Authority, and Conversion content, you stop chasing followers and start building an engaged community that actually buys. The post includes a visual breakdown chart showing the recommended posting ratio and what each content type achieves.

## Key Insights & Main Ideas

- **Insight 1:** There are 4 essential content pillars for social media growth: Growth, Connection, Authority, and Conversion — each with a distinct goal and format.
- **Insight 2:** Growth content should make up the largest share of your posts (~50%), followed by Connection (~35%), Authority (~35%), and Conversion (~40%) based on the visual chart weightings.
- **Insight 3:** Each content type maps to a specific audience outcome: Growth = new people find you, Connection = people like you, Authority = people trust you, Conversion = people take action.
- **Insight 4:** The strategy is designed to move strangers through a funnel — from discovery to loyalty to purchase — using content alone.

## Detailed Breakdown / Step-by-Step

**Slide 1 — Cover:** "4 types of content I would create to grow on social media" — sets the hook with a lifestyle photo and bold typography. Subtitled "most powerful types of content."

**Slide 2 — Growth Content (aka Reach Content):**
- **Goal:** New people find you.
- **Purpose:** Gain more followers and become a go-to person in your niche.
- **Examples:** Tutorials, how-to guides, step-by-step guides, tips/hacks/secrets, lists/tools/resources, answering FAQs in your niche.

**Slide 3 — Connection Content (Make Them Feel Something):**
- **Goal:** Make people like you.
- **Purpose:** Give people hope, motivation, and that little push to take action.
- **Examples:** Sharing your journey, encouraging people to step outside their comfort zone, highlighting client transformations, talking about mindset shifts and lessons learned.
- **Featured sticky note:** "It's never too late to become the person you've always wanted to be."

**Slide 4 — Authority Content (Credibility Content):**
- **Goal:** Prove you know your stuff.
- **Purpose:** Build trust through results and social proof.
- **Examples:** Results, proof of work, before and after, case studies, breakdowns.
- **Visual:** A retro phone showing "10K+ followers" and 198K engagement as proof.

**Slide 5 — Conversion Content (Because You're Not Just Here for Fun):**
- **Goal:** Make people take action.
- **Purpose:** Attract the right people to your offer and grow your business.
- **Examples:** Sharing methods & frameworks, freebies & lead magnets, showcasing your products/services, breaking down how to work with you, client testimonials.
- **Affirmation shown:** "I don't chase, I attract. What belongs to me will simply find me."

**Slide 6 — Visual Chart Breakdown:**
- A chart showing what each post type does for you, with a percentage-style bar visualization.
- Full breakdown table:
  - **Growth:** Brings strangers to your page | Tips, tutorials, anything shareable | Reach & saves
  - **Connection:** Turns followers into fans | Behind the scenes, honest takes | Comments & replies
  - **Authority:** Proves you know your stuff | Results, client wins, case studies | DMs & trust
  - **Conversion:** Asks people to do something | Freebie, product, booking link | Clicks & sales

**Slide 7 — CTA Slide:** Shows a content calendar tool (Notion-based) with posts scheduled for the week. Encourages viewers to "comment EASY" to receive free video lessons on creating 6 pieces of content in less than 1 hour.
