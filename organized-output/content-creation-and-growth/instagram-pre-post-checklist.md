## Executive Summary

This reel delivers a concise, actionable checklist designed to be performed right before hitting "Post" on Instagram. The creator argues that what you do in the minutes before publishing — engaging with others, posting to Stories, choosing trending audio, timing your post, and reviewing your content — directly influences how many people the algorithm serves your content to. ## Core Insights & Takeaways

- **Insight 1:** Algorithm warm-up matters — engaging with other accounts before posting signals activity to Instagram and can boost initial distribution.
- **Insight 2:** Stories act as a visibility primer — posting to Stories keeps you top-of-mind with existing followers, increasing the chance they engage with your feed post when it drops.
- **Insight 3:** Trending audio is a discovery lever — using popular sounds increases the likelihood of your reel being surfaced to non-followers via Explore and Reels feeds.
- **Insight 4:** Posting time is a visibility multiplier — publishing when your audience is most active gives your content the best chance at early engagement, which feeds the algorithm.
- **Insight 5:** Pre-publish review catches conversion killers — typos, weak hooks, and missing CTAs silently tank performance. A final check prevents easily avoidable mistakes.
