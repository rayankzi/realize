## Core Insights & Takeaways

- **Insight 1:** Enable your content to appear in Google Searches — this expands discoverability beyond the Instagram platform itself, tapping into organic search traffic.
- **Insight 2:** Turn off the Snooze feature — snoozing can suppress your content's distribution; disabling it ensures your posts remain active in the algorithm's rotation.
- **Insight 3:** Turn on Translation Settings — this allows your content to reach non-native-language audiences, broadening your potential viewer base internationally.
- **Insight 4:** Check your Account Status regularly — ensures there are no hidden restrictions, shadowbans, or policy violations limiting your reach.
- **Insight 5:** Allow anyone to follow you — removing follow restrictions reduces friction for new audience members discovering your content.
- **Insight 6:** Allow GIF comments — enabling richer engagement formats (like GIFs) can boost comment activity, which signals the algorithm to distribute your content more.
- **Insight 7:** Fix your Content Preferences — adjusting these settings helps Instagram's algorithm better categorize and recommend your content to the right audiences.
