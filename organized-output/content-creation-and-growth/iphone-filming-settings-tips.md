## Core Insights & Takeaways

- **Insight 1:** Film in 4K at 30fps with enhanced stabilisation on — this is the optimal iPhone setting for clear, steady social media content.
- **Insight 2:** Enable the grid and level composition tools to ensure you're always centered and your camera is straight, eliminating crooked or off-center shots.
- **Insight 3:** Instagram has a hidden "upload at highest quality" toggle in media quality settings — without it enabled, your carefully filmed 4K footage gets compressed.
- **Insight 4:** Always use the native iPhone camera app rather than filming directly within Instagram or TikTok, which apply their own compression during recording.
- **Insight 5:** Natural daylight works but limits filming windows; a ring light or clip-on phone light removes that constraint.

## Narrative & Step-by-Step Breakdown

1. **Hook (Before/After comparison):** The creator opens with a visual comparison — polished content vs. low-quality content — to immediately demonstrate the stakes.
2. **Camera Settings:** Navigate to Settings > Camera > Record Video. Set to **4K at 30fps**. Turn on **Enhanced Stabilisation**. Turn everything else off.
3. **Composition Tools:** Still in Camera settings, scroll to Composition and enable **Grid** and **Level** to keep shots centered and straight.
4. **Lighting:** Use natural daylight if possible, but invest in a **ring light** or **clip-on phone light** for flexibility to film anytime.
5. **Instagram Upload Setting:** Go to Instagram Settings > Media Quality and toggle on **Upload at Highest Quality**.
6. **Filming Hygiene Tips:**
   - Always film with the **back camera** (not the selfie camera).
   - **Clean the lens** before every filming session.
   - Film using the **native iPhone Camera app**, not within Instagram or TikTok.
