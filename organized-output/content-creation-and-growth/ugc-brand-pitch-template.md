## Core Insights & Takeaways

- **You don't need to be an influencer:** UGC is content created for brands to post on *their* accounts, not yours — so follower count is irrelevant.
- **Proactive outreach is essential:** Brands will not find you on their own. You need to search for them and pitch first using Instagram or TikTok's search features (e.g., "skincare Ireland," "beach hotels Italy").
- **A simple DM pitch works:** The creator shares the exact template they use to land free and paid collaborations, proving that a straightforward, professional message can open doors.
- **Build a brand list first:** Before pitching, compile a targeted list of brands in your niche using platform search tools.
