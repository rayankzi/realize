## Core Insights & Takeaways

- **Insight 1:** Virality follows a repeatable pattern—it's not random luck. A disciplined hook → reveal → captions structure reliably generates views *and* followers.

- **Insight 3:** Retention is destroyed when you answer the hook immediately. Delaying the reveal until the end of the video keeps viewers watching, which directly improves algorithmic distribution.
- **Insight 4:** Most viewers consume content on mobile in noisy or public environments (airports, trains, restrooms), making readable on-screen captions essential for accessibility and retention.
