- **Experience fuels your interview performance.** The projects and roles you take on outside of class become concrete talking points during interviews, giving you stories and examples to draw from.
- **Start now, even from zero.** The creator openly admits they had nothing outside of school three years prior — the message is that it's never too late to begin building practical experience.
