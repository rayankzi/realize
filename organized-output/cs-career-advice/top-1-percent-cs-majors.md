## Core Insights & Takeaways

- **Insight 1:** Knowing how to code is table stakes — you must also know how to deploy and scale applications. DevOps knowledge (Jenkins, Argo CD) is a critical differentiator.
- **Insight 2:** Git proficiency beyond basic push/pull (branching, rebasing) combined with AI-assisted "vibe coding" creates a speed advantage that employers notice.
- **Insight 3:** Soft skills matter more than most CS students think. Interviews are as much about cultural fit as technical ability — humor and passion make you memorable.
- **Insight 4:** Linux and command-line skills are a dying art but remain highly in demand. Deep OS knowledge (virtual memory, inodes, kernel messages) can be the deciding factor in a big tech interview.
