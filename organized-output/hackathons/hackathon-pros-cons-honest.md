## Executive Summary

This reel delivers a candid breakdown of hackathon culture from someone who's attended four. Rather than the typical "just go for it" hype, the creator pairs each benefit with a real downside — you'll ship fast but get humbled hard, you'll get noticed but destroy your sleep, and the networking is elite but the imposter syndrome is brutal. The caption reinforces the core message: you'll never feel ready, so stop waiting.

## Core Insights & Takeaways

- **Insight 1:** Hackathons are the best forcing function for shipping — you build in 24 hours what most people spend months thinking about, even if it's incomplete.
- **Insight 2:** Your first hackathon will humble you significantly. Imposter syndrome is real and rarely discussed, but pushing through it is part of the growth.
- **Insight 3:** Hackathon projects can create unexpected career opportunities — the creator's last project "blew up overnight" and opened doors that wouldn't have existed otherwise.
- **Insight 4:** The networking value is massive. Fellow participants, judges, and booth visitors can become future engineers, hiring managers, or recruiters. The creator nearly landed their first internship through a hackathon networking booth.
- **Insight 5:** Sleep deprivation is inevitable. Despite repeated promises to avoid all-nighters, the creator pulled one at every single hackathon.
