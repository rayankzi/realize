## Executive Summary

This carousel by Abhijeet presents a comprehensive, pattern-organized list of 100 must-do DSA (Data Structures & Algorithms) questions for coding interview preparation. Rather than a random assortment, the problems are grouped into 13 fundamental algorithmic patterns — from Arrays & Hashing to 2D Dynamic Programming — with each problem tagged by difficulty (Easy, Medium, Hard) and linked to its LeetCode page. This structured approach enables candidates to systematically build competency across all major interview topics.

## Key Insights & Main Ideas

- **Pattern-based learning:** The 100 problems are organized into 13 distinct patterns, enabling focused study of one technique at a time rather than random problem-solving.
- **Balanced difficulty curve:** Each pattern starts with easier problems and progresses to harder ones, building foundational understanding before tackling complex variations.
- **Complete interview coverage:** The 13 patterns span the full spectrum of topics commonly tested at top tech companies — arrays, trees, graphs, dynamic programming, and more.
- **Concise and actionable:** Each entry includes the problem name, difficulty level, and a direct LeetCode link, making it immediately actionable for study.
