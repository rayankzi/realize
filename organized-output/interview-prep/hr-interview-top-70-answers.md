## Verbatim Templates & Scripts

> **Q1: Tell me about yourself**
> I recently completed my degree in [your field] from [college/university]. During my academic journey, I developed strong interest in areas like [mention relevant skills or subjects]. I worked on projects where I learned how to apply theoretical knowledge to real-world problems. I enjoy learning new technologies and improving my problem-solving skills. I am currently looking for an opportunity where I can contribute my skills while continuing to grow professionally.

> **Q2: Why do you want to work for this company?**
> I want to work for this company because of its reputation for innovation and strong work culture. The company's focus on delivering quality solutions and continuous improvement aligns with my personal values. I am particularly interested in how the company approaches technology and problem-solving. I believe working here will also allow me to learn from experienced professionals while contributing my skills to meaningful projects.

> **Q3: Why should we hire you?**
> You should hire me because I bring the right combination of technical knowledge, willingness to learn, and dedication to work. I am someone who takes responsibility seriously and strives to deliver quality results. I also work well in teams and communicate effectively with others. I am confident that my enthusiasm and problem-solving approach will allow me to contribute to the organization.

> **Q5: What are your weaknesses?**
> One weakness I noticed earlier was that I sometimes spent too much time trying to perfect small details in my work. Over time, I realized that balancing perfection with efficiency is important. Now I prioritize tasks better and focus on completing work effectively within deadlines while still maintaining quality.

> **Q7: Why did you leave your previous job?**
> I learned a lot in my previous role and gained valuable experience. However, I am now looking for new challenges that will help me expand my skills and grow professionally. I want to work in an environment where I can contribute more significantly and continue learning.

> **Q43: What value can you bring to this company?**
> I can bring dedication, problem-solving ability, and a strong willingness to learn. I believe in delivering quality work and maintaining a positive attitude even when facing challenges. I also [continues].
