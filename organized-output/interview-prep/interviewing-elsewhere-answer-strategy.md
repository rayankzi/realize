# How to Answer "Are You Interviewing Elsewhere?" to Speed Up Your Hiring Process

**Topic/Category:** Career & Interview Strategy
**Primary Goal of the Reel:** Teach job seekers how to strategically answer the common recruiter question "Are you interviewing elsewhere?" to create urgency, validate their market value, and accelerate their hiring timeline.

---

## Executive Summary

This reel breaks down why recruiters ask "Are you interviewing elsewhere?" and why your answer should always be "yes." The creator — a former job seeker who interviewed at companies like TikTok, DoorDash, and Robinhood, and who is now a hiring manager — explains that saying yes triggers two powerful responses: recruiters prioritize you as a candidate and compress your interview timeline. He shares the exact email template he used to leverage competing processes and move things forward faster.

## Core Insights & Takeaways

- **Insight 1:** Recruiters ask "Are you interviewing elsewhere?" primarily to understand your timeline and gauge whether other companies value you — it's a signal-checking question, not a trick.
- **Insight 2:** Saying "yes" creates urgency. Recruiters will prioritize you and shorten your process because they don't want to lose a candidate other companies are competing for.
- **Insight 3:** Framing competing offers while expressing genuine interest in the company ("your company is really where I want to work") is the ideal balance — it creates urgency without seeming uncommitted.
- **Insight 4:** This dynamic is confirmed from both sides: the creator experienced it as a candidate and now sees it play out as a hiring manager.
