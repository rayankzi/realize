# 7 Claude AI Prompts to Supercharge Your LinkedIn Profile

**Topic/Category:** Career / LinkedIn Optimization
**Primary Goal of the Post:** Provide 7 ready-to-use AI prompts that help job seekers optimize every section of their LinkedIn profile without needing LinkedIn Premium or paid tools.

---

## Executive Summary

This carousel presents a practical alternative to LinkedIn Premium by offering 7 specific Claude AI prompts that cover every major section of a LinkedIn profile. The prompts address headline optimization, About section rewriting, experience bullet ROI framing, full-profile audits, keyword injection for recruiter search ranking, unique value proposition creation, and elevator pitch generation. The core message is that free AI tools can replace expensive subscriptions when used with the right prompts.

## Key Insights & Main Ideas

- **Insight 1:** LinkedIn Premium is unnecessary if you know how to leverage AI prompts to optimize your profile for recruiter search and ATS systems.
- **Insight 2:** Each prompt targets a specific LinkedIn section (headline, about, experience, skills, featured) with a distinct optimization goal — there's no one-size-fits-all prompt.
- **Insight 3:** The prompts emphasize recruiter-facing language: keywords, KPIs, outcomes, and positioning — not generic self-descriptions.
- **Insight 4:** A strong Unique Value Proposition (UVP) at the top of the About section is highlighted as a key differentiator most people miss.
- **Insight 5:** Turning your profile into a 30-second pitch doubles as content for video intros and summary sections.

## Verbatim Templates & Scripts

**Prompt 1 — LinkedIn Headline That Sells:**
> "You are a world-class LinkedIn personal branding expert. Rewrite my LinkedIn headline to make it highly attractive to recruiters in my field. Use clear keywords, job-specific value, and a positioning that makes me irresistible in search. Here's my profile for context: [PASTE LINKEDIN PROFILE]"

**Prompt 2 — Optimize Your About Section:**
> "You are a top-tier LinkedIn copywriter. Rewrite my 'About' section so that it clearly explains who I am, what problems I solve, and why I'm valuable while keeping it warm, clear, and keyword-optimized. Keep it under 260 words. Here's my existing profile: [PASTE PROFILE TEXT]"

**Prompt 3 — Rewrite Experience to Show ROI:**
> "You are a resume and LinkedIn optimization specialist. Rewrite my 'Experience' section for each job listed in my LinkedIn so it focuses on outcomes, numbers, KPIs, and skills recruiters look for. Make sure it's easy to skim and speaks in recruiter language. Here's my current experience section: [PASTE TEXT]"

**Prompt 4 — Fix Your LinkedIn Profile Instantly:**
> "You are a professional LinkedIn profile optimizer for Fortune 500 job seekers. Review my entire LinkedIn profile below and suggest exact rewrites for headline, about, experience, skills, and featured sections. Make sure it's fully keyword-optimized for ATS and recruiter search. My goal: [Insert goal]. Here's the profile: [PASTE PROFILE TEXT]"

**Prompt 5 — Inject Keywords To Rank Higher:**
> "You are a LinkedIn SEO expert. Scan my profile and inject high-ranking keywords for my industry (e.g., data science, UX, etc.) in a natural way to make it rank higher in recruiter search. Do not keyword stuff. My profile: [PASTE PROFILE TEXT]"

**Prompt 6 — Build a Unique Value Proposition:**
> "You are a personal branding strategist. Based on my experience and career goals, write a bold, specific Unique Value Proposition that I can put at the top of my 'About' section to instantly hook recruiters and differentiate me. Here's my experience and industry: [PASTE DETAILS]"

**Prompt 7 — Turn Your Profile Into a Pitch:**
> "You are a career storyteller and elevator pitch expert. Based on my LinkedIn profile, write a 30-second pitch that I can use in my summary section or video intro. Make it sharp, confident, and results-driven. Profile: [PASTE PROFILE TEXT]"
