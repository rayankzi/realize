# LinkedIn DM Templates That Actually Get Responses

**Topic/Category:** Job Search / Cold Outreach / Networking
**Primary Goal of the Reel:** Share three ready-to-use LinkedIn direct message templates for different networking scenarios to help job seekers (especially international students) start conversations that lead to referrals and interviews.

---

## Executive Summary

This reel provides three plug-and-play LinkedIn DM templates tailored to different outreach scenarios: reaching out to someone on the hiring team, responding to a "we're hiring" repost, and cold-messaging when no role is posted. The creator — a Cornell engineering graduate and Martech PM at Lenovo — draws from personal experience to show that effective outreach doesn't require an existing connection or referral, just a clear, respectful, and relevant message. The content is especially targeted at international students on F-1/OPT visas navigating the U.S. job market.

## Core Insights & Takeaways

- **Insight 1:** Tailor your DM based on the recipient's relationship to the role — someone on the hiring team gets a different message than someone who simply reposted a job listing.
- **Insight 2:** Every template follows a consistent structure: **Introduction → Credential/Context → Specific Ask → Resume Link**. This makes messages scannable and action-oriented.
- **Insight 3:** You don't need a referral to start a conversation — cold outreach works when you lead with relevance and respect, not desperation.
- **Insight 4:** Including a resume link (e.g., via TinyURL) removes friction and lets the recipient evaluate your fit immediately without extra back-and-forth.
