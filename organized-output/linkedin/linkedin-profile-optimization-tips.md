# 12 Steps to Fix Your Dead LinkedIn Profile

**Topic/Category:** Career Development / LinkedIn Optimization
**Primary Goal of the Reel:** Provide a comprehensive, actionable checklist for optimizing a LinkedIn profile to attract recruiters and land jobs in 2026.

---

## Executive Summary

This reel delivers a dense, 12-step LinkedIn profile overhaul guide aimed at job seekers whose profiles are underperforming. The caption serves as the primary content vehicle, walking viewers through everything from algorithm tricks (toggling "Open to Work") to crafting human-sounding About sections and using the XYZ bullet format for experience entries. The creator positions these tips as essential for anyone serious about landing a job in 2026, emphasizing that most profiles leave easy wins on the table.

## Core Insights & Takeaways

- **Trigger LinkedIn's algorithm** by toggling your "Open to Work" settings off and back on, and set visibility to "Recruiters only" to avoid the green banner.
- **Your headline is prime real estate** — format it as "[Role] | [Skills] | [Value Prop]" to immediately communicate what you offer.
- **Use the XYZ format for experience bullets** — "Accomplished X by doing Y, resulting in Z" — to quantify your impact and stand out.
- **Visual proof beats text** — upload media files (presentations, projects, articles) to each experience entry.
- **Engagement matters more than passive optimization** — post weekly about lessons learned, leave substantive comments, and personalize connection requests.
- **The Featured section is underused** — pin your best posts, articles, and projects right below your headline.

## Narrative & Step-by-Step Breakdown

The reel is structured as a numbered checklist in the caption, delivering all 12 steps sequentially:

1. **Toggle "Open to Work"** off and back on to re-trigger LinkedIn's algorithm. Use "Recruiters only" visibility.
2. **Design a custom banner in Canva** with your name, target role, 2-3 key skills, and email. 90% of profiles leave this blank.
3. **Optimize your headline** with skills and a value proposition. Example: "Marketing Manager | SEO, Content | Helping B2B Brands Convert."
4. **Humanize your About section** — open with a hook, tell your story, include a personal detail, and close with contact info.
5. **Use the XYZ bullet format** for experience. Example: "Increased engagement 45% by implementing content calendar, gaining 10K followers."
6. **Upload media files** to each experience entry — presentations, projects, articles for visual proof.
7. **Reorder skills** for relevance and actively request endorsements from connections for specific skills.
8. **Change your location** to your target city, since recruiters filter by location. Be upfront about relocation plans.
9. **Personalize connection requests** — write 2-3 sentences referencing something specific from the person's profile.
10. **Post weekly** about lessons learned and struggles. Vulnerability and learning posts outperform pure achievement posts.
11. **Leave valuable comments** — share specific examples, ask questions, and offer context in 2-3 sentences instead of generic reactions.
12. **Curate your Featured section** — pin top posts, articles, and projects for immediate visibility.
