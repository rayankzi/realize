# How to Get Leads Using Claude Code + Cold Emails

**Topic/Category:** Marketing / Sales Automation / AI Tools
**Primary Goal of the Post:** Demonstrate how to build an automated cold email lead generation system using Claude Code and Claude Skills (MCP) that handles campaign ideation, copywriting, review, and deployment — reducing what used to take half a day to ~4 minutes.

---

## Executive Summary

This carousel by Suprava Sabat walks through a complete AI-powered cold emailing system built with Claude Code and Claude Skills. The system automates the entire cold email campaign lifecycle — from ideation and copywriting to campaign deployment inside a sending platform (Leadfly). The key selling point is dramatic time savings: what previously took hours or half a day now takes approximately 4 minutes, while producing 6+ campaign variations with full email sequences, spin-tax variations, and ready-to-launch formatting. The human stays in the loop only for review and approval.

## Key Insights & Main Ideas

- **Claude Skills for Marketing:** The system uses specialized Claude Skills (marketing-research, social-content, creative-designer, data-analysis, campaign-presenter) to power different parts of the cold email workflow.
- **End-to-End Automation:** The pipeline covers ideation, copywriting, human review, and automatic campaign creation — the user only needs to review and click approve.
- **Massive Time Savings:** The system generates 6 campaigns with 3 email sequences each (plus spin-tax variations) in ~4 minutes, replacing what used to take half a day.
- **Self-Improving System:** The system learns over time by analyzing replies, campaign performance, and winning angles to create better campaigns in future iterations.
- **Real Results:** Dashboard metrics shown: 2,281 total leads contacted, 223 unique replies (9.78% reply rate), 32 unique positive replies (14.35% positive reply rate).
