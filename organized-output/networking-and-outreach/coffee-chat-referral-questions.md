## Verbatim Templates & Scripts

### Career Clarity Questions
> 1. How/why did you decide to pursue this career?
> 2. What other roles or industries would you consider if you were to pivot today?
> 3. How has this career actually impacted your lifestyle (hours, flexibility, etc.)?

### Role + Team Dynamics Questions
> 1. What does your work environment look like (team structure, cross-functional work, etc.)?
> 2. How do you typically split your time between strategy vs execution?
> 3. Who are the main stakeholders you work with regularly?

### Industry Questions
> 1. What developments are most impacting this field right now?
> 2. Are there more opportunities or more competition in this space today?
> 3. What has changed the most in this industry over the past few years?

### Skills + Standing Out Questions
> 1. What tools, platforms, or skills are most important to learn right now?
> 2. How do most people successfully break into this field today?
> 3. Based on my background, what can I improve on to be a stronger fit for [specific role] on your team?

*Note: The creator specifically calls out question 3 in the Skills category as the one that has led to referrals.*
