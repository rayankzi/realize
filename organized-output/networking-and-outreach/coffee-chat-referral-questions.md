## Core Insights & Takeaways

- **Referral questions should come at the end of the chat:** The creator explicitly advises saving the referral-oriented questions for the end of the conversation, after you've built rapport and demonstrated genuine interest.
- **Ask questions that make it easy for someone to refer you:** Questions like "What would you want to see from a candidate before feeling comfortable referring them?" let the other person set the bar — and then you can meet it.
- **Understand your positioning before applying:** Several questions focus on identifying gaps, strengths, and how to frame your background, which is critical prep work before submitting applications.
- **Team-level questions show genuine interest:** Asking about team goals, success metrics, and culture signals that you're evaluating fit — not just begging for a job.
- **Domain-specific questions (growth marketing) add depth:** Tailoring questions to your specific field (e.g., "How much do you value channel expertise vs strategic thinking vs analytics?") shows you've done your homework.

## Verbatim Templates & Scripts

### 🤝 Referral Questions (Save for End of Chat)

> 1. Based on my background, do you think I'd be a strong fit for roles like yours or on your team?
> 2. What would you want to see from a candidate before feeling comfortable referring them?
> 3. Is there anything in my experience or resume I should tighten up before I start applying?
> 4. Are there specific teams, roles, or hiring managers at your company I should keep an eye on?
> 5. If I apply, is there a best way to position my background for those roles?
> 6. If you think I'd be a fit, would you feel comfortable referring me?

### 🎯 Questions to Understand Your Fit

> 7. From your perspective, what parts of my background seem strongest?
> 8. Where do you think I may have gaps compared with other candidates?
> 9. How would you position someone with my background in today's market?
> 10. What job titles should I realistically be targeting right now?
> 11. What would make a hiring manager more confident in someone with my profile?
> 12. Are there certain keywords, skills, or accomplishments I should make more clear on my resume?

### 🏢 Questions to Understand the Team

> 13. What are the main goals your team is focused on right now?
> 14. How is success measured on your team?
> 15. What qualities stand out most in top performers there?
> 16. What makes someone successful on your team beyond just technical skills?
> 17. What type of person tends to thrive at your company?
> 18. What are the biggest challenges your team is trying to solve right now?

### 📈 Growth Marketing-Specific Questions

> 19. For growth marketing roles today, what skills feel most valuable in 2026?
> 20. Where do you see the biggest gap between average candidates and strong candidates?
> 21. How much do you value channel expertise vs strategic thinking vs analytics?
> 22. For growth or performance roles, which metrics matter most to leadership?
> 23. How important is experimentation and testing in your team's workflow?
> 24. How is AI actually changing the way your team works day to day?
