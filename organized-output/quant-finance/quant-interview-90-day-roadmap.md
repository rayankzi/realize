## Narrative & Step-by-Step Breakdown

This is an informational reel structured as a numbered roadmap:

1. **Probability (Daily Practice):** Solve 5–10 interview-style probability questions daily. Focus on conditioning, expectation tricks, symmetry, order vs counting, and information gain. Redo problems until you can explain the logic out loud without notes. Track weak topics in a notebook and revisit weekly.

2. **Python (Project-Based Learning):** Build small trading projects immediately following the pipeline: data download → signal generation → backtest → performance report. Implement volatility targeting, simple momentum, and factor regression before touching deep learning. Write clean functions, test assumptions, plot everything.

3. **Market Structure (Conceptual Understanding):** Study how order books work, what market makers optimize, how risk is managed, and why correlations change in crises. Learn about execution, liquidity, and portfolio construction. Focus on understanding the problems quants actually solve.

4. **Feedback Loops (Deliberate Practice):** Attempt problems under time pressure, review mistakes, rewrite solutions cleanly, then teach the idea in one minute. This forces intuition development through reflection, not passive consumption.

5. **Consistency (90-Day Commitment):** Commit to one probability problem set, one Python feature, and one market concept every single day. Small daily progress compounds into interview readiness.
