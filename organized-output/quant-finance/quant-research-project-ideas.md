## Narrative & Step-by-Step Breakdown

The reel presents a listicle format, walking through 10 quant research project ideas:

1. **Probability of Backtest Overfitting** — Detect when trading strategies are merely curve-fitted to historical data rather than genuinely predictive.
2. **Bayesian Black-Litterman Portfolio Optimization** — Enhance classical portfolio allocation by incorporating Bayesian updates for better uncertainty management.
3. **Survival-Based Risk Prediction** — Use upper and lower partial moments to forecast risk in a more realistic way than traditional variance-based methods.
4. **Model-Free Reinforcement Learning for Factor Portfolios** — Deploy an RL agent to optimize factor exposures without relying on explicit model assumptions.
5. **Statistics of Sharpe Ratios** — Study the reliability and variability of Sharpe ratios across different strategies to understand what "good performance" actually means statistically.
6. **Systematic Portfolio Trading via Convex Optimization** — Optimize multi-period trades while accounting for transaction costs and risk constraints.
7. **Time-Series vs Cross-Sectional Factor Analysis** — Compare how carry, momentum, and value strategies perform when analyzed across time vs. across assets, especially in different market regimes.
8. **Numeraire-Invariant Quadratic Hedging** — Explore hedging and portfolio optimization techniques that don't require assuming the existence of a risk-free asset.
9. **Pricing Exotic Derivatives & Model Risk** — Value complex options (barriers, lookbacks, etc.) and quantify the uncertainty introduced by model assumptions.
10. **Regime-Switching Factor Strategies** — Adapt factor exposures (carry, momentum, value) dynamically based on detected market regime changes.

**Conclusion:** The creator closes by encouraging the viewer to pick one idea, replicate it, and add it to their quant portfolio.
