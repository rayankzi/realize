## Executive Summary

This reel delivers six structured AI prompts designed to overhaul a resume end-to-end. Each prompt targets a specific weakness most resumes share: vague positioning, task-based bullet points, missing ATS keywords, passive language, and poor visual hierarchy. The creator packages these as plug-and-play templates where users paste their resume and a target role to get immediate, actionable rewrites. The caption serves as the primary content vehicle, with the video itself acting as a visual hook to drive viewers to read and save it.

## Core Insights & Takeaways

- **Insight 1:** Recruiters spend roughly 10 seconds scanning a resume — your most impressive achievements must be immediately visible at the top.
- **Insight 2:** Responsibility-based bullet points ("Managed a team…") are far weaker than achievement-based ones with real metrics and business impact.
- **Insight 3:** ATS keyword alignment is critical but must be done naturally — keyword stuffing will get flagged or rejected.
- **Insight 4:** Executive-level language (concise, confident, no filler) signals seniority regardless of your actual experience level.
- **Insight 5:** A strong professional summary should clearly state who you are, what you specialize in, and what results you deliver — not generic fluff.
- **Insight 6:** Each prompt is designed to be used sequentially, building a complete resume overhaul pipeline.

## Narrative & Step-by-Step Breakdown

The reel presents six prompts in a logical progression that mirrors the resume-writing process:

1. **Brutal Recruiter Audit** — Start by getting a harsh, realistic assessment of your current resume's weaknesses from a recruiter's perspective.
2. **Positioning and Personal Brand Reset** — Rewrite your professional summary to clearly define your value proposition for a target role.
3. **Achievement Conversion** — Transform every generic responsibility into a quantified, result-driven bullet point.
4. **ATS Keyword Alignment** — Cross-reference your resume with a specific job description to close keyword gaps.
5. **Executive Tone Upgrade** — Elevate the language to sound senior, confident, and precise.
6. **10-Second Scan Optimization** — Restructure the layout so your strongest points are immediately visible.

## Verbatim Templates & Scripts

**Prompt 1 — Brutal Recruiter Audit:**

> "Act as a senior recruiter screening 300 resumes daily. Review my resume and tell me exactly why it would be ignored in 10 seconds. Flag weak positioning, vague impact, and structural issues. Resume: [paste]. Target role: [insert role]."

**Prompt 2 — Positioning and Personal Brand Reset:**

> "Rewrite my professional summary to clearly position me for [target role]. Define who I am, what I specialize in, and what results I consistently deliver. Keep it sharp, specific, and impossible to ignore."

**Prompt 3 — Achievement Conversion:**

> "Convert every responsibility in my resume into a measurable achievement. Replace task-based statements with result-driven bullet points using real metrics, outcomes, and business impact. Resume: [paste]."

**Prompt 4 — ATS Keyword Alignment:**

> "Compare my resume with this job description and identify every missing keyword. Rewrite my resume to align naturally with the role without keyword stuffing. Resume: [paste]. Job description: [paste]."

**Prompt 5 — Executive Tone Upgrade:**

> "Rewrite my resume using concise, confident, executive-level language. Cut filler words, passive phrases, and generic statements while keeping everything accurate and honest. Resume: [paste]."

**Prompt 6 — 10-Second Scan Optimization:**

> "Reformat my resume so my most impressive achievements and strongest skills are visible within the first 10 seconds of reading. Improve structure, visual hierarchy, and overall clarity. Resume: [paste]."
