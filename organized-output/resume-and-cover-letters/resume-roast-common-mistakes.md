## Executive Summary

The creator reviews a follower's software engineering resume, assigning penalty points for each mistake. The resume scores a brutal 2 out of 10 and would not pass a resume screen. ## Core Insights & Takeaways

- **Insight 1:** Always include your location on your resume — recruiters need to know where you're based.
- **Insight 2:** Include your GPA under education if it's competitive; omitting it raises red flags.
- **Insight 3:** Every experience entry should have at least 4 bullet points. If you have fewer than 2 experiences total, you need to find ways to build more (clubs, internships).
- **Insight 4:** Use a specific bullet point formula for experience entries (implied: action verb + what you did + result/impact).
- **Insight 5:** Projects should each have 4 bullet points with the same structured format — a wall of text guarantees no recruiter will read it.
- **Insight 6:** Keep the technical skills section clean and standard — don't add random or unclear items at the bottom.
