## Narrative & Step-by-Step Breakdown

This is an informational/educational reel structured as a listicle in the caption:

- **Hook:** The caption opens with a bold claim — "Agentic AI is the hottest space in tech right now" and a challenge — "most candidates can't even explain the basics."
- **Core Content (15 Concepts):**
  1. **Tool Use / Function Calling** — Agents using external tools (APIs, search, databases) to take real actions.
  2. **ReAct Pattern** — Think → Act → Observe → Repeat loop.
  3. **Multi-Agent Orchestration** — Multiple specialized agents collaborating on a task.
  4. **Human-in-the-Loop** — Human approval before agent execution.
  5. **Agent Memory (Short-term vs Long-term)** — Conversation context vs. cross-session memory.
  6. **Guardrails & Safety Layers** — Rules preventing harmful or off-track behavior.
  7. **Planning & Task Decomposition** — Breaking complex tasks into manageable steps.
  8. **MCP (Model Context Protocol)** — Standard for agent-tool connectivity.
  9. **A2A (Agent-to-Agent Protocol)** — Standard for inter-agent communication across teams/companies.
  10. **Agent Evaluation & Benchmarking** — Measuring real-task performance rigorously.
  11. **Reflection & Self-Correction** — Agent reviewing and fixing its own output.
  12. **Context Window Management** — Strategically managing limited model input.
  13. **RAG (Retrieval Augmented Generation)** — Pulling external documents to inform responses.
  14. **Agent Handoffs & Routing** — Passing tasks between agents based on requirements.
  15. **Observability & Tracing** — Tracking every agent step for debugging.
