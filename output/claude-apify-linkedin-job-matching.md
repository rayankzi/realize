# How to Use Claude + Apify to Match LinkedIn Jobs to Your Resume

**Topic/Category:** Tech Tutorial / Job Search Automation
**Primary Goal of the Reel:** Demonstrate how to connect Claude AI with Apify to scrape LinkedIn job postings and automatically score them against your resume for targeted cold applications.

---

## Executive Summary

This reel walks through a streamlined workflow for automating job application targeting. By connecting Claude AI with Apify (a web scraping platform), users can scrape the latest LinkedIn job postings and have Claude analyze each job description against their resume. The output is a ranked list of jobs with fit scores, helping job seekers focus their cold applications on positions where they have the highest chance of being shortlisted. The creator emphasizes simplicity — the entire process requires just one prompt after initial setup.

## Core Insights & Takeaways

- **Insight 1:** Claude excels at cross-referencing job descriptions with your resume to produce a meaningful "fit score," saving hours of manual comparison.
- **Insight 2:** The entire workflow is reduced to a single prompt after the one-time setup of connecting Apify to Claude — no coding or complex configuration required.
- **Insight 3:** This process should be repeated daily when cold applying, but the creator advises that cold applying should not be your primary job search strategy — use this to maximize ROI when you do cold apply.
- **Insight 4:** Apify handles the LinkedIn scraping side, while Claude handles the intelligence layer (resume matching and scoring), making the two tools complementary.

## Narrative & Step-by-Step Breakdown

1. **Hook/Setup:** The creator introduces the end goal — a table or list showing fit scores for scraped LinkedIn jobs matched against your resume.
2. **Connect Apify to Claude:** Sign up on Apify, navigate to the integrations tab, and connect it to Claude. The creator mentions this is straightforward and offers to send detailed instructions via DM.
3. **Upload Your Resume:** Within Claude, upload your resume so it has context about your skills, experience, and qualifications.
4. **Paste the Prompt:** Use a single prompt (provided by the creator via DM) that instructs Claude to analyze the scraped job postings against your resume.
5. **Review Results:** Claude outputs a ranked list of jobs you should apply to, scored by how well your resume matches each position.
6. **Repeat Daily:** Run this process each day during your job search to stay on top of new postings and apply strategically.
