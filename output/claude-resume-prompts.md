**Topic/Category:** Career / Job Search / AI-Powered Resume Optimization
**Primary Goal of the Post:** To share 5 specific AI prompts you can use with Claude to dramatically improve your resume, tailor it to job descriptions, and land more interview callbacks.

## Executive Summary

This carousel post presents a bold claim — feeding your resume to Claude AI and using 5 strategic prompts resulted in 15 interview calls in just 7 days. The post walks through a complete resume optimization pipeline: from brutally honest self-assessment, to job description keyword matching, to rewriting bullet points with the X-Y-Z formula, to bridging skills gaps, and finally generating a role-specific action plan with portfolio projects and certifications. The prompts are designed to be used sequentially as part of a job application workflow.

## Key Insights & Main Ideas

- **AI as a Resume Coach:** Claude can act as a brutally honest hiring manager, ATS optimizer, and career strategist — all in one tool.
- **The X-Y-Z Bullet Formula:** Structuring resume bullets as "Accomplished X, by doing Y, which resulted in Z" makes them specific, quantified, and impactful.
- **ATS + Human Optimization:** The prompts specifically target both automated ATS screening and the human recruiter's 6-second scan, covering both sides of resume filtering.
- **Gap Bridging Strategy:** Rather than ignoring skill gaps, the approach suggests micro-projects and certifications that can be completed in 2 weeks to fill them.
- **Role-Specific Tailoring:** Every application should be customized — the prompts encourage mapping your resume against each specific job description.

**Prompt 2: The JD Decoder**

> Here is a job description [paste JD] and here is my resume [paste resume].
>
> Extract the top 10 skills and keywords this employer actually cares about.
>
> Now map each one against my resume and tell me where I match, where I'm weak, and what exact phrases I should add to pass both ATS screening and a human recruiter's 6-second scan.

**Prompt 3: The Bullet Rewriter**

> Rewrite these experience and project bullet points from my resume [paste bullets] using the X-Y-Z formula: Accomplished X, by doing Y, which resulted in Z. Make them specific, quantified, and tailored to this role [paste JD]. If I haven't mentioned numbers, suggest realistic metrics I could include based on the type of work.

**Prompt 4: The Skills Gap Fixer**

> Here's the job description [paste JD] and my resume [paste resume]. I know I don't meet every requirement. Identify the exact gaps, then suggest 2-3 micro-projects or certifications I can realistically complete in 2 weeks that would fill each gap. Also rewrite my resume summary to honestly position me as a fast learner who's actively bridging these gaps.

**Prompt 5: The Role-Specific Resume Generator**

> Here is the job description [paste JD], my resume [paste resume], and my current skill set [list skills]. I have 2 weeks before the interview. Give me a complete action plan:
>
> List 2 portfolio projects I can build from scratch that directly demonstrate the skills this role demands. Each project should be completable in 2-3 days, impressive enough to discuss in an interview, and hosted on GitHub with a proper README.
>
> Suggest the top 2 free/affordable certifications (Google, IBM, Coursera, or any credible Indian platform) that would instantly strengthen my profile for this exact role. Tell me which ones can be completed fastest and which carry the most weight with Indian recruiters.
>
> For each project and certification, give me the exact line I should add to my resume and the talking point I should prepare for the interview to explain why I did it.
