# Top 6 Claude Skills Every Designer Needs

**Topic/Category:** Design Tools / AI for Designers
**Primary Goal of the Post:** Introduce designers to Claude's hidden "Skills" plug-in system and showcase six essential skills that enhance design-to-code workflows, branding, theming, and creative output.

---

## Executive Summary

Claude has a lesser-known plug-in system called "Skills" that most designers are unaware of. This carousel by Adrien Ninet walks through six curated skills that transform how Claude assists with design work — from enforcing a full design-thinking process before writing code, to converting Figma files into production-ready code, to generating real visual art as exportable files. Each skill is installable via a single `npx` command, making adoption frictionless.

## Key Insights & Main Ideas

- **Skills are simple plug-ins** that change how Claude approaches design tasks — they're not complex configurations, just installable behavior modifiers.
- **Front-End Design skill** forces Claude through a full design thinking process before writing any code, preventing generic AI-generated output ("AI slop").
- **Figma to Code** enables 1:1 fidelity conversion from Figma designs to production-ready code by simply pasting a Figma URL.
- **Theme Factory** provides 10 ready-to-use professional themes with curated color palettes and font pairings, saving designers from starting from scratch.
- **Brand Guidelines** auto-enforces your brand's colors, fonts, spacing, and tone across everything Claude outputs — ensuring brand consistency at scale.
- **Canvas Design** goes beyond code and creates actual visual art, posters, and compositions as real PNG/PDF files.
- **Skill Creator** lets you build your own custom skills tailored to your design system, brand voice, or workflow.
