## Narrative & Step-by-Step Breakdown

**This is a tutorial/recipe. Here are the steps:**

1. **Prepare the latte base:** In a cup or pitcher, combine about 1 tsp chocolate sauce and 1 tbsp caramel sauce.
2. **Add espresso:** Pour a double shot of espresso over the syrups.
3. **Mix well:** Stir the espresso and syrups together thoroughly.
4. **Add milk:** Pour in 6-8 oz of your preferred milk to cool down the espresso. Use as much as you think will fit in your cup.
5. **Make the chocolate cold foam:** In a separate container, combine 3 tbsp heavy cream, 2 tbsp milk, about 1/2 tbsp cocoa powder, and 1 tbsp vanilla syrup.
6. **Froth:** Froth the cold foam mixture until it's thick and airy.
7. **Build the drink:** Fill your serving cup with plenty of ice.
8. **Pour the latte base** over the ice.
9. **Top with chocolate cold foam:** Pour the frothed chocolate foam on top.
10. **Enjoy:** Give it a taste and admire how good it looks.
