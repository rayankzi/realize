# The High Performer Interview Question You Need to Ask

**Topic/Category:** Career & Interview Strategy

## Executive Summary

The creator shares a go-to interview question they've battle-tested across many corporate interview settings: asking the interviewer what a "high performer" looks like in the role. The premise is simple — most candidates can do the job, but this question signals that you're aiming higher than baseline competency. It reframes the conversation from "can you do the work?" to "how do I excel here?" while also extracting valuable intel about what the company truly values.

## Core Insights & Takeaways

- **Insight 1:** The "high performer question" is a strategic move that sets you apart from other qualified candidates by signaling ambition and a growth mindset.
- **Insight 2:** The question works because it implicitly acknowledges competition ("you're going to interview a lot of people who can do the job") while redirecting focus to excellence rather than adequacy.
- **Insight 3:** After asking the question, you stop talking — the silence is deliberate. It gives the interviewer space to reveal what truly matters to them, which you can then mirror in your responses.
