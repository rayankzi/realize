## Executive Summary

This reel highlights three go-to websites for job hunting: Glassdoor for company research and salary data, NewGrad-Jobs for entry-level and mid-level role discovery, and Jobright.ai as an all-in-one AI-powered job tool. ## Core Insights & Takeaways

- **Insight 1:** Don't apply for jobs directly through Glassdoor — use it for employee reviews and salary ranges instead, then apply on the company's actual website for better results.
- **Insight 2:** NewGrad-Jobs.com is an excellent aggregator for entry and mid-level roles across many career fields. Turning on notifications to apply early gives you a competitive edge.
- **Insight 3:** Jobright.ai offers job matching, tailored resumes, and recruiting profile optimization. The creator credits it with helping them land their full-time role after tailoring their recruiting profile and seeing increased interview callbacks.
