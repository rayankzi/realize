## Core Insights & Takeaways

- **Insight 1:** Coffee chats are not just networking — they serve as a research tool to discover roles you might be interested in and can directly lead to referrals.
- **Insight 2:** The highest response rates come from personalized messages that reference specific common ground (same school, org, major, background, or identity), not cold generic asks.
- **Insight 3:** Before reaching out, study the person's LinkedIn profile — their background, posts, and experience — to find a genuine connection point.
- **Insight 4:** When pivoting careers (e.g., agency to tech), intentionally target people who made the same transition for the most relevant advice.

**Alumni Outreach Template:**

**Org/Club Connection Template:**

> Hi xx! I saw you were involved in [Club/Org] as well — would love to hear how that experience helped shape your career.

**Personalization Categories to Research Before Reaching Out:**
- Same school / org / sorority / frat
- Similar major or career path
- Similar background or identity
