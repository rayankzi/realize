# 12 LinkedIn Fixes to Stop Losing Job Opportunities

**Topic/Category:** Career / LinkedIn Optimization
**Primary Goal of the Reel:** Provide a comprehensive, actionable checklist for optimizing a LinkedIn profile to attract recruiters and land jobs in 2026.

This reel delivers a dense, 12-step playbook for reviving a "dead" LinkedIn profile. The caption carries the entire instructional payload — covering everything from algorithm hacks (toggling "Open to Work") to content strategy (weekly struggle posts) and networking etiquette (personalized connection requests). ## Core Insights & Takeaways

- **Insight 1:** Toggling "Open to Work" off and back on re-triggers LinkedIn's algorithm, and setting visibility to "Recruiters only" avoids the public green banner.
- **Insight 2:** The XYZ bullet format ("Accomplished X by doing Y, resulting in Z") is the recommended structure for experience entries — it forces quantifiable results into every line.
- **Insight 3:** Engagement quality matters more than frequency — leaving substantive 2-3 sentence comments with specific examples outperforms generic reactions like "Great post!"
- **Insight 4:** Visual proof (uploaded media, Featured section pins) dramatically strengthens a profile because most users leave these sections blank.
- **Insight 5:** Authentic "struggle posts" about lessons learned outperform polished achievement posts for weekly content.

## Narrative & Step-by-Step Breakdown

The reel follows a numbered listicle format. Here is the full breakdown:

1. **Re-trigger the algorithm** — Turn "Open to Work" off then on again. Set to "Recruiters only" to skip the green banner.
2. **Design a custom banner in Canva** — Include your name, target role, 2-3 key skills, and email. 90% of profiles leave this blank.
3. **Optimize your headline** — Format: `[Role] | [Skills] | [Value Prop]`. Example: "Marketing Manager | SEO, Content | Helping B2B Brands Convert."
4. **Humanize your About section** — Start with a hook, tell your story, add one personal detail, end with contact info. Write conversationally.
5. **Use XYZ format for experience bullets** — "Accomplished X by doing Y, resulting in Z." Example: "Increased engagement 45% by implementing content calendar, gaining 10K followers."
6. **Upload media to experience entries** — Add presentations, projects, articles as visual proof.
7. **Reorder skills & get endorsements** — Put job-relevant skills first. Message connections asking them to endorse specific skills.
8. **Change location to your target city** — Recruiters filter by location. Be upfront about relocation plans.
9. **Personalize connection requests** — Write 2-3 sentences mentioning something specific from the person's profile. No generic messages.
10. **Post weekly about lessons learned** — Share what went wrong and what you learned. Struggle posts beat achievement posts.
11. **Leave valuable comments** — Share specific examples, ask questions, offer context in 2-3 sentences instead of "Great post!"
12. **Curate your Featured section** — Pin top posts, articles, and projects. This section appears right after your headline.
