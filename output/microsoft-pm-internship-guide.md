**Slide 3 — Productify Resume:** Use Jake's resume template for scannability. Add product keywords and frame experience like a PM would — "Shipping features" and "Drove X project to get Y outcome."

**Slide 4 — Mock Interviews:** Completed 25 product interview mocks with peers and actual PMs at Microsoft and other big tech companies. Start interview prep earlier than expected. Use a spreadsheet tracker and focus on improving one thing per mock for compounding growth.
