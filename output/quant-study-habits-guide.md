## Core Insights & Takeaways

- **Insight 1:** Practise interview-style problems (probability puzzles, mental maths, market-making games) rather than passively reading textbooks. Simulating the interview environment early builds the reasoning and communication skills that actually get tested.
- **Insight 2:** Depth beats breadth. Mastering core areas (probability, statistics, linear algebra, optimisation, market microstructure) produces compounding returns, while shallow coverage crumbles under interview pressure.
- **Insight 3:** Strategic project selection matters — build things that showcase decision-making under uncertainty (backtests, execution simulators, volatility models), prioritizing reasoning and risk control over flashy code.
- **Insight 4:** Actively review mistakes by keeping error logs and revisiting failed problems weeks later. This builds the pattern recognition that is the single biggest differentiator in quant interviews.
- **Insight 5:** Optimise for consistency over intensity — two focused hours daily outperforms sporadic 10-hour cram sessions and mirrors how actual trading desks operate.
